Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Your privacy matters. This page explains what QR Studio does — and does not — do with your information.

The content you enter

When you generate a QR code, the link or text you type is sent to our server only to render the corresponding image, which is returned to your browser immediately. We do not store the content of your QR codes, associate it with you, or use it for any other purpose.

Information we collect

We do not require an account and we do not ask for personal information. Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically record standard technical logs (such as IP address, browser type and time of request) for security and reliability. These logs are not used to identify individual visitors.

Cookies

QR Studio itself does not set tracking cookies. However, third-party services such as our advertising partners may set cookies as described below.

Advertising and Google AdSense

We may display advertisements served by Google, including through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, see Google's Partner Sites policy.

Third-party links

QR codes you create may point to external websites. Once you follow such a link, this Privacy Policy no longer applies, and we encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Children's privacy

QR Studio is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@qr-studio.app.
