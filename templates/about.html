About QR Studio

A free, fast and privacy-friendly way to turn any link or text into a clean, high-resolution QR code.

What QR Studio does

QR Studio takes any URL, message, Wi-Fi detail, or plain text you enter and instantly renders it as a scannable QR code. You can customise the foreground and background colours to match your brand, then download the image as a PNG or copy it straight to your clipboard. There are no accounts, no watermarks and no limits — paste, generate, and you are done.

Why we built it

Most online QR generators bury the tool under sign-up walls, upsells or low-quality exports. We wanted the opposite: a single, focused page that produces crisp, print-ready codes in a couple of seconds and gets out of your way. QR Studio is that tool — useful for posters, business cards, restaurant menus, event check-ins, product packaging, or simply sharing a link across the room.

How it is made

The site is built with Flask on the server and the widely-used open-source qrcode library, which generates every code with high error correction so it stays reliable even when printed small or partially obscured. The interface is a lightweight, hand-written front end with no heavy frameworks, so it loads fast on any device.
