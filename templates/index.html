Frequently asked questions

The quick answers to what people ask most.

What can I turn into a QR code?

Any URL, plain text, phone number, email address or Wi-Fi login. If it fits in text, it fits in a QR code.

Do my QR codes ever expire?

No. The codes are static — they encode your data directly, so they keep working forever and never depend on our servers.

Are my links stored or tracked?

No. The text you enter is used only to render the image and is not saved. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I change the colours?

Yes — pick any foreground and background colour. Just keep enough contrast between them so scanners can read the code reliably.

Is it really free?

Yes. QR Studio is free to use with no limits. Ads on the page help keep it that way.
